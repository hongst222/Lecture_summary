### **컴퓨터 용량 단위**
```
8bit --> 1byte

1024byte --> 1kbyte

1024kb --> 1mb

1024mb --> 1gb

1024gb --> 1tb

1024tb --> 1pb

1024pb --> 1eb

1024eb --> 1zb

1024zb --> 1yb
```
